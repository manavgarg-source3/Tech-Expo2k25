About
Tech Expo'25

From the spark of the first invention to the marvels of modern technology, human ingenuity has always been the force that drives progress. HMR Techfest 2025 proudly presents its theme: "Igniting Tomorrow"—a celebration of the relentless pursuit of knowledge, discovery, and transformation.
Innovation is not just about change; it's about shaping the future. From the invention of the printing press to the rise of artificial intelligence, from the first flight to the dream of interplanetary travel—every breakthrough has been fueled by curiosity and a vision for a better tomorrow.
This year's edition is bigger and better, featuring hackathons, coding competitions, AI workshops, robotics challenges, and interactive panel discussions led by industry experts. Whether you're a tech wizard or just stepping into the digital world, Techfest 2025 has something for everyone!
